Healthy World Special: Broccoli and Cheddar soup

This healthy Broccoli and Cheddar soup is loaded with nutritious veggies, and cheese. It is one of the most popular soups in the world. It is known for its creamy texture and cheesy flavor, and is very simple to make.



Ingredients:

  • broccoli florets and stem cut into small bite-size pieces
  • Optional: 1 cup finely grated, cut, or julienned carrots.
  • 2 cups of cheddar cheese, grated
  • Other Veggies as per individual preference


Directions:

  1. Add broccoli, and other veggies and simmer
  2. blend broccoli, set a little bit aside to add texture at the end
  3. Stir in cream mixed with flour, simmer a couple of minutes. Stir in cheeses and serve
